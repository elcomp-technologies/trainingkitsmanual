RS-232/Serial Port Development board Front layout

1. B-01b. I/O & Power LEDs
  • YELLOW (TOP LED): Serial Data Out (to host)
  • GREEN (MIDDLE LED): Serial Data In (from host)
  • RED (BOTTOM LED): POWER
2. B-01c. Serial-Port
  • Standard DB-9 (RS-232) connector
3. B-01d. RSSI LEDs
  • 3 LEDs ON = Very Strong Signal (> 30dB fade margin)
  • 2 LEDs ON = Strong Signal (> 20 dB fade margin)
  • 1 LED ON = Moderate Signal (> 10 dB fade margin)
  • 0 LED ON = Weak signal (< 10 dB fade margin)
4. B-01e. Power Connector
  • VDC Power Connector

USB Development board


1. B-11a. I/O & Power LEDs
  • YELLOW (TOP LED) = Serial Data Out (to host)
  • GREEN (MIDDLE LED) = Serial Data In (from host)
  • RED (BOTTOM LED) = POWER
2. B-11b. RSSI LEDs
  • 3 LEDs ON = Very Strong Signal (> 30 dB fade margin)
  • 2 LEDs ON = Strong Signal (> 20 dB fade margin)
  • 1 LED ON = Moderate Signal (> 10 dB fade margin)
  • 0 LED ON = Weak signal (< 10 dB fade margin)
3. B-11c. USB Port
  • Standard Type-B connector is used to communicate with host and power RF module
